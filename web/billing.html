Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Monthly subscriptions can be canceled with 30 days notice. Annual subscriptions are non-refundable but won't auto-renew if canceled.

Do sponsors control the project?

No. All sponsors are appreciated but have no control over project decisions. See our GOVERNANCE.md for details.

Do you provide invoices?

Yes! GitHub Sponsors provides automatic invoices. For direct payments, we'll send invoices upon request.

What if I can't afford sponsorship?

No problem! Contributing code, documentation, or community support is equally valued. The tools are free and open source for everyone.

How are funds used?

50% development, 25% infrastructure, 15% community, 10% operations. Full transparency in our governance documents.

Questions about billing or sponsorship?

Email: funding@cyberai.network
