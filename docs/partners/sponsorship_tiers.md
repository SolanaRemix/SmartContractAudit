# Sponsorship Tiers

Financial sponsorship helps sustain CyberAi development, infrastructure, and community. We offer multiple tiers to accommodate different levels of support.

- **Independence**: Sponsorship does NOT grant control over project decisions
- **Transparency**: All sponsors publicly listed
- **Community First**: Open source community remains our priority
- **Tax**: We are not currently a registered non-profit; consult your tax advisor

See [GOVERNANCE.md](/GOVERNANCE.md) for sponsor influence policy.

## Sponsorship Terms

### Duration
- **Monthly**: Cancel anytime with 30 days notice
- **Annual**: 12-month commitment, auto-renews unless canceled 60 days before renewal

### Recognition
- Added within 5 business days of first payment
- Remains for duration of sponsorship plus 30 days
- Removed upon cancellation or non-payment

### Feature Requests
- Subject to community approval
- Must align with project goals
- No guarantee of implementation
- Timeline based on maintainer capacity
- Publicly tracked like all features

### Support
- Response time targets (not guarantees unless custom SLA)
- Bronze: 5 business days
- Silver: 3 business days
- Gold: 1 business day
- Platinum: 4 hours (business hours)

### Changes
- Tier benefits may change with 60 days notice
- Price increases not applied to existing sponsors for 12 months
- New sponsors get current pricing and benefits

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can we sponsor anonymously?**  
A: Yes, for Bronze and Silver. Gold+ requires public recognition for transparency.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for non-profits?**  
A: Yes, 50% discount for registered non-profits and educational institutions. Contact us for details.

**Q: Can we sponsor a specific feature?**  
A: Yes! Custom sponsorship. Feature must have community approval.

**Q: What if we can't afford sponsorship but want to help?**  
A: Community sponsorship (contributions) is equally valued! Code, docs, and support are invaluable.

**Q: Can we cancel anytime?**  
A: Monthly sponsors can cancel anytime. Annual sponsors committed for the year but won't auto-renew if canceled.

**Q: Do you provide invoices?**  
A: Yes, automatic for GitHub Sponsors and Open Collective. Manual invoices for direct payments.

**Q: What currency do you accept?**  
A: USD primary. Can accommodate EUR, GBP, and cryptocurrency.
